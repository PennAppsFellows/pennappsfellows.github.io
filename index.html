About

PennApps Fellows is a 10 week summer internship for students who want to explore and get to know the Philly tech scene.

Program Features




Free Housing

We take care of housing so you can worry about building the next Facebook. SF and NYC can be expensive, here in Philly, we got your back. Past locations include Intl. House Philly and The Axis.


Fellows Events

Get to know your fellow Fellows: Phillies games, fancy dinners downtown, museums, Six Flags, movie nights, mini-hackathons, you name it, we'll help you do it.


Exploring Philadelphia

Visit all parts of Philly from Old City resturants to Center City museums, we have you covered. Get to know awesome local groups like Code for Philly.

These companies are the backbone of the thriving startup community in Philly.
